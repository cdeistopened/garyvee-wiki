# $1.80 Daily Tracker & Compliance Coach

Track whether you're actually completing the daily $1.80 discipline: 90 meaningful comments across 10 hashtags x 9 top posts. Provide accountability, flag when engagement quality slips, and measure whether the strategy is producing real growth.

## Context

The $1.80 strategy is a daily discipline. Most people try it for 3 days, get bored, and quit. Or they do it for a month but let quality slip into "nice post!" territory. This skill exists to keep you honest — tracking both quantity AND quality.

> "If you do that every day, you're going to grow." — Financial Freedom Channel, explaining GaryVee's strategy (`g6pAYKkvSac`)

> "The goal is to give people value and give them a reason to follow you by commenting on posts. Consistency is key." — Zach Bordeaux (`04fJ79qT22Q`)

> "If you keep doing this every day you're going to grow a lot faster. I used it when I was first starting out on Instagram and it built that momentum." — Jeremiah (`Ip83aSz2BJM`)

The math is the structure: 10 hashtags x 9 top posts x $0.02 (your two cents) = $1.80. But the magic is in the consistency and quality compounding over weeks and months.

## The Consultation

### Step 1: Gather Today's Engagement Log

Ask:
1. **How many hashtags did you engage with today?** (Target: 10)
2. **How many total comments did you leave?** (Target: 90)
3. **What time did you start and finish?** (Tracking efficiency)
4. **Paste your 3 best comments from today.** (Quality check)
5. **Paste your 3 weakest comments from today.** (Honesty check)
6. **Did any of your comments get replies?** (Signal tracking)
7. **Did you get any new followers today?** (Growth tracking)
8. **Any DMs received from people you engaged with?** (Deep signal)
