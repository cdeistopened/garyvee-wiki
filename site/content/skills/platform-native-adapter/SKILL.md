# Platform-Native Adapter

Take a single micro content nugget and produce platform-specific briefs for every target platform. Gary's core principle: respect the audience, respect the platform, make your agenda third.

## Context

This is Gary's sharpest tactical insight. The same content cannot be posted identically everywhere. Each platform has its own psychology, its own audience mindset, its own native format.

> "I know a 40-year-old woman is in a different mindset when she's on Facebook versus when she's on Pinterest. On Pinterest, she has intent to shop, aspiration to shop, and on Facebook, she's keeping up with her world or consuming information. Respecting the audience, respecting the platform, taking your agenda and making it third." — Gary Vaynerchuk, AskGaryVee #38 (`yhZDv3dwFyM`)

> "Take the same video and upload it directly to Facebook... all those platforms want you to stay on their platform. If you take a video from YouTube and take that raw link and put it on Facebook, nobody sees it." — Kevin J Pino, Content Model breakdown (`FvUhZQfG350`)

## The Consultation

### Step 1: Understand the Nugget

Ask:
1. **What's the content nugget?** (A clip, a quote, a story, a tactical tip — describe it)
2. **What format is it currently in?** (Video clip, written text, audio, image)
3. **Which platforms do you want to hit?** (Or default to Gary's full 7: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts, Facebook, LinkedIn, Snapchat Spotlight, X/Twitter)
4. **What's the goal?** (Brand awareness, drive to website, grow followers, sell something)
5. **Who's your audience on each platform?** (Age, profession, interests — may differ by platform)

### Step 2: Apply the Platform Psychology Model

For each target platform, adapt using Gary's platform-audience framework:

| Platform | Audience Mindset | Native Format | Content Psychology |
|----------|-----------------|---------------|-------------------|
| **TikTok** | Discovery + entertainment. Scrolling fast. Short attention. | Vertical video 15-60s, trending audio, text overlays | Hook in first 1 second. Energy matters. Raw > polished. Trending formats help discovery. |
| **Instagram Reels** | Aspirational + social. Slightly more polished expectation. | Vertical video 15-90s, carousels (up to 20 slides), stories | Visual quality matters more than TikTok. Carousels drive saves. Stories for behind-the-scenes. |
| **YouTube Shorts** | Learning + discovery. Feeds Gemini AI (AEO/GEO). | Vertical video 15-60s | Critical for AI search indexing. Descriptive titles matter. Educational content performs. |
| **Facebook** | Keeping up with world. 45-80 demo massively undervalued. | Video (any orientation), long-form text, images | The most underpriced attention right now per Gary. Longer content works. Share-friendly format. |
| **LinkedIn** | Professional development. B2B. Thought leadership. | Text posts (1300 char), carousels (PDF), native video | Lead with insight, not hype. Professional framing. Vulnerable personal stories crush. |
| **Snapchat Spotlight** | 15-35 demo. Entertainment. Untapped by marketers. | Vertical video 15-60s | Almost zero competition from brands. Pure entertainment plays. |
| **X / Twitter** | Real-time conversation. Hot takes. Threads for depth. | Text (280 char), threads, short video, quote images | Punchy first line. Threads for tactical content. Engagement bait works. |
| **Substack / Beehiiv** | Deep reading. Intentional audience. High trust. | Long-form written (1000-3000 words) | Written expansion of the nugget. Journalistic depth. |
| **Pinterest** | Intent to shop, plan, aspire. Evergreen discovery. | Vertical images, infographics, idea pins | Evergreen tactical content. How-to's. Lists. Searchable titles. |

> "YouTube Shorts has never been more important because the content you put out on YouTube Shorts is feeding the Gemini LLM, which is going to allow your product to show up when people type in 'I need a [service]. Who should I go with?'" — Gary Vaynerchuk, Expo West 2026 (`VMumHwVEfFs`)

> "Facebook proper, not Instagram, one could argue is the most important platform right this second because of how much attention is actually on it and how many people have forgotten about it." — Gary Vaynerchuk, Day Trading Attention interview (`_TdzXnYJuSY`)

### Step 3: Build Platform-Specific Briefs

For each platform, produce a brief with:

1. **Format**: Exact content type (vertical video, carousel, text post, etc.)
2. **Dimensions/Specs**: Technical specifications
3. **Hook**: The first 1-3 seconds or first line (platform-specific)
4. **Body**: How the content plays out on this specific platform
5. **CTA**: Call to action appropriate to the platform (comment, share, follow, link in bio)
6. **Hashtags/Tags**: Platform-specific tagging strategy
7. **Caption/Copy**: The accompanying text
8. **Native Upload Note**: Always upload natively. Never cross-post links.

### Step 4: Prioritize

Not every nugget needs to hit every platform. Recommend a priority order based on:
- Where the audience for this specific content lives
- Which platforms the nugget's format naturally suits
- Where competition is lowest (Snapchat Spotlight, Facebook Blue are currently underpriced)

## Output

Deliver a **Platform Adaptation Brief** in this format:

```markdown
# Platform Adaptation Brief
**Nugget:** [One-sentence description]
**Original Format:** [video clip / text / audio]

## TikTok
- **Format:** Vertical video, [X] seconds
- **Hook (first 1s):** [Exact opening text overlay or spoken words]
- **Body:** [How the content plays]
- **Caption:** [Full caption with hashtags]
- **CTA:** [Specific call to action]
- **Audio:** [Original / trending sound / voiceover]
- **Priority:** [High/Medium/Low for this platform]

## Instagram (Reels + Feed)
- **Reels Format:** [specs]
- **Carousel option?** [Yes/No — if yes, outline slides]
- **Hook:** [platform-specific]
- **Caption:** [Full caption]
- **Hashtags:** [5-15 relevant]
- **CTA:** [action]

## YouTube Shorts
- **Format:** Vertical, [X]s
- **Title:** [Descriptive, keyword-rich for Gemini/AEO]
- **Hook:** [first 3s]
- **Description:** [keyword-optimized]
- **Why this matters for AEO:** [brief note on AI discoverability]

## Facebook
- **Format:** [Video / text post / image]
- **Hook:** [first line of text or video]
- **Caption:** [longer-form OK on Facebook]
- **Share framing:** [Why someone would share this]

## LinkedIn
- **Format:** [Text post / carousel PDF / video]
- **Professional angle:** [How to frame for B2B audience]
- **Hook:** [first line]
- **Full copy:** [text]

## X / Twitter
- **Format:** [Single tweet / thread / video]
- **Tweet 1:** [exact text, under 280 chars]
- **Thread structure:** [if applicable]

## Snapchat Spotlight
- **Format:** Vertical video
- **Adaptation notes:** [entertainment-forward framing]

## Production Checklist
- [ ] Cut video to platform specs
- [ ] Add text overlays per platform
- [ ] Write all captions
- [ ] Upload natively to each platform (NO cross-posting links)
- [ ] Schedule per distribution-orchestrator timing
```
