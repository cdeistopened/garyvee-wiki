# Ask GaryVee — Social Media Strategy & Content Engine Coach

An AI coach built from Gary Vaynerchuk's books (Jab, Jab, Jab, Right Hook; Crushing It!; Day Trading Attention), podcast, and social content. Not a chatbot impersonating Gary — a structured library of real frameworks, real quotes, and real decision tools extracted from his work. Every recommendation traces back to a specific source.

## Quick Start

Ask any question about social media, content creation, platform strategy, or audience growth:
- "Audit my last 15 posts"
- "Which platforms should I focus on?"
- "How do I get 30 pieces of content from one video?"
- "Am I selling too much on social?"
- "Help me with the $1.80 strategy"
- "Why aren't my posts getting views?"

Or use a specific skill directly:

### Playbook 1: Content Pyramid (5 skills)
Turn one long-form piece into 30+ pieces of platform-native content.

- `/ask-garyvee:pillar-content-identifier` — Find your best long-form content to repurpose
- `/ask-garyvee:micro-content-extractor` — Extract 15-30+ micro pieces from one pillar
- `/ask-garyvee:platform-native-adapter` — Adapt each piece for each platform's native format
- `/ask-garyvee:distribution-orchestrator` — Plan release timing and sequence
- `/ask-garyvee:community-listener` — Use engagement signals to drive Round 2 content

### Playbook 2: Day Trading Attention (5 skills)
Find underpriced attention and amplify your winners.

- `/ask-garyvee:platform-arbitrage-scanner` — Find where attention is cheapest right now
- `/ask-garyvee:platform-gap-analyzer` — Identify platforms you're missing
- `/ask-garyvee:hook-auditor` — Win the first second of every piece of content
- `/ask-garyvee:post-performance-scorer` — Measure relative performance to find overindexers
- `/ask-garyvee:winner-amplification-tool` — Turn organic winners into paid amplification

### Playbook 3: $1.80 Strategy (3 skills)
Build audience through strategic engagement before you have content scale.

- `/ask-garyvee:hashtag-mapper` — Find your 10 target hashtags
- `/ask-garyvee:comment-crafter` — Write comments that build real connections
- `/ask-garyvee:daily-180-tracker` — Track your daily engagement routine

### Playbook 4: Jab-Jab-Jab-Right-Hook (3 skills)
Get the give/ask ratio right so your audience stays engaged.

- `/ask-garyvee:content-ratio-auditor` — Audit your jab/hook ratio
- `/ask-garyvee:selfless-selfish-audit` — Check if your "value" content is actually selfish
- `/ask-garyvee:right-hook-optimizer` — Make your sales posts convert

### Playbook 5: Content Audit (3 skills)
Full diagnostic when things aren't working.

- `/ask-garyvee:fifteen-post-diagnostic` — The flagship 15-post comprehensive audit
- `/ask-garyvee:daily-content-calendar-builder` — Build a daily posting cadence across 7 platforms
- `/ask-garyvee:content-kill-iterate-engine` — Decide what to kill vs. iterate

## The Key Difference: GaryVee's Specific Frameworks

Gary doesn't deal in vague "post more" advice. This plugin preserves his precision:

| Generic AI Response | Ask GaryVee Response |
|--------------------|---------------------|
| "Post consistently" | "100 pieces of content a day across 7 platforms. Not 3 posts a week. The Content Pyramid: 1 pillar piece yields 30+ micro pieces. You document, you don't create." |
| "Engage with your audience" | "$1.80 strategy: leave your two cents on the top 9 posts for 10 relevant hashtags. That's 90 meaningful comments per day. Not 'nice post' — real two cents." |
| "Find the right platform" | "Day trade attention. Right now, LinkedIn organic reach is underpriced for B2B. TikTok is underpriced for discovery. The mispricing changes quarterly." |
| "Don't sell too much" | "Jab jab jab right hook. 3:1 minimum. But even your jabs can be selfish — the selfless/selfish audit catches value content that's really humble-bragging." |
