# Hashtag Niche Mapper

Identify the 10 hashtags in your niche that have the right volume, audience overlap, and community tone for the $1.80 Instagram engagement strategy. These 10 hashtags become your daily engagement territory.

## Context

The $1.80 strategy only works if you pick the right 10 hashtags. Too broad (e.g., #fitness with 500M posts) and your comments drown. Too narrow (e.g., #kettlebellsforseniors with 200 posts) and there's nobody to engage with. The sweet spot is hashtags where YOUR target audience congregates and where the top 9 posts get meaningful engagement.

> "Compile the top 10 hashtags that are relevant for my Instagram account... each day I'll search that hashtag and I can see the top nine posts that are related to that hashtag. So I go into each post and I leave a thoughtful comment." — Financial Freedom Channel, explaining GaryVee's strategy (`g6pAYKkvSac`)

> "Pick 10 trending hashtags in your niche. Find the top nine people on that hashtag and comment something of value on their post." — Zach Bordeaux, explaining the $1.80 strategy (`04fJ79qT22Q`)

## The Consultation

### Step 1: Understand Their Niche

Ask:
1. **What's your business or creator niche?** (Be specific — not "fitness" but "home workouts for busy parents")
2. **Who is your ideal audience?** (Age, interests, problems they have, platforms they use)
3. **What 3-5 hashtags do you currently use on your own posts?** (This reveals their current positioning)
4. **Who are 3-5 accounts in your space that you admire or compete with?** (We'll mine their hashtag usage)
5. **What's your engagement capacity?** (Can you do all 90 comments daily, or do you need to start with 5 hashtags x 9 posts = 45?)

### Step 2: Build the Hashtag Map

For each candidate hashtag, evaluate:

| Criteria | What to Look For | Red Flag |
|----------|-----------------|----------|
| **Post volume** | 100K-5M total posts (sweet spot) | Under 10K (dead) or over 50M (ocean) |
| **Top 9 quality** | Posts from real creators, not bots or brands | All sponsored posts or stock images |
| **Engagement on top 9** | Comments with real conversations, not just emojis | Ghost town or all bot comments |
| **Audience overlap** | People in the top 9 comments match your target demo | Comments from accounts outside your target |
| **Community tone** | Supportive, educational, conversational | Toxic, spammy, or purely transactional |
| **Your expertise fit** | You can add genuine value in comments here | You'd have to fake knowledge to comment |

**The Three Tiers:**

- **Core hashtags (4-5):** Directly in your niche. Your expertise is obvious. You could comment in your sleep.
- **Adjacent hashtags (3-4):** Related niches where your audience overlaps. You bring a fresh perspective.
- **Aspirational hashtags (1-2):** Slightly bigger communities where you want to build presence. Stretch territory.

### Step 3: Score and Rank

Rate each candidate hashtag 1-5 on:
- **Audience match** — Are these MY people?
- **Comment opportunity** — Can I add value others aren't providing?
- **Engagement quality** — Are real conversations happening?
- **Volume fit** — Not too big, not too small?

Total score out of 20. Top 10 make the cut.

### Step 4: Map the Community per Hashtag

For each of your 10 selected hashtags, identify:
- **3-5 frequent posters** — These become your recurring engagement targets
- **Common question themes** — What do people ask about in comments?
- **Knowledge gaps** — What questions go unanswered? (This is your opening)
- **Community norms** — Long comments or short? Formal or casual? Emoji-heavy or text-focused?
