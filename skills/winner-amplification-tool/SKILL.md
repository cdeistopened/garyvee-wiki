# Winner Amplification Tool

When a post overindexes organically, this skill tells you exactly what to do with it: amplify as-is, send it down-funnel as a performance ad, or send it up-funnel to brand campaigns. This is where organic content strategy meets paid media — the core of Day Trading Attention.

## Context

Gary's revolutionary thesis: for 70 years, media dollars were used to hide bad creative. Now, media dollars should amplify good creative that has already been validated organically.

> "For 70 years, working media dollars were used to hide bad creative. Now, when you put out all your advertising on the organic algorithms, when something does remarkable, that's when you should put media dollars behind it because you've gotten validation that people think it's good. So now I believe the job of working media dollars is to amplify good creative. We've gone from big money hiding bad creative to now big money amplifying good creative." — Gary Vaynerchuk, Day Trading Attention interview (`_TdzXnYJuSY`)

> "When something gets 57,000 views, knowing what to do with that creative — sending it down to the lower funnel and making that a performance ad, sending it up if you've got the budgets to CTV or making it the campaign or even the commercial." — Gary Vaynerchuk, Expo West 2026 (`VMumHwVEfFs`)

> "The days of taking and making a video or an ad and running media on Facebook or Instagram or Tik Tok to get clients are over. You should always post every piece of content you make for a sale or an ad organically first. If it does well in views compared to your norm, that's what you start running media on." — Gary Vaynerchuk, chiropractic keynote (`Sio8lkFXiMY`)

## The Consultation

### Step 1: Validate the Winner

Before spending any money, confirm this is a real overindexer:

1. **What post overindexed?** (Link or description)
2. **What's your norm?** (Average views for this platform over last 30 days)
3. **What's the overindex ratio?** (Post views / norm views)
4. **What engagement signals?** Beyond views:
   - Comment sentiment (positive, curious, argumentative?)
   - Save rate (high saves = reference value = converts well to ads)
   - Share rate (high shares = emotional resonance)
   - Completion rate for video (high completion = strong content)
5. **What's your ad budget available?** (Range: $50 test to $50K+ campaign)
6. **What's the business objective?** (Brand awareness, leads, direct sales, followers)

### Step 2: Determine the Amplification Path

Three paths based on Gary's midfunnel model:

**Path A: Boost As-Is (Mid-Funnel)**
- **When:** Overindex ratio 3-5x, content is already in a format the platform can amplify (video, carousel)
- **What you do:** Put paid media behind the exact organic post
- **Budget range:** $50-500 to start, scale based on results
- **Targeting:** Start with lookalike audiences of your existing followers, then broaden
- **Expected outcome:** 3-10x the organic reach, lower CPM than cold creative

**Path B: Convert to Performance Ad (Lower Funnel)**
- **When:** Overindex ratio 5x+, content has a natural product/service tie-in, business can handle leads/sales
- **What you do:** Edit the organic content into a direct-response ad. Add a clear CTA. Retarget warm audiences.
- **Budget range:** $500-5,000 initial test
- **Targeting:** Retarget people who engaged with the organic post + lookalikes of engagers
- **Expected outcome:** Measurable ROAS, lead generation, direct sales

**Path C: Elevate to Brand Campaign (Upper Funnel)**
- **When:** Overindex ratio 10x+, content captures the brand's core message, budget exists for CTV/programmatic
- **What you do:** Use the organic content as the basis for a full campaign. Produce polished variants. Run on CTV, YouTube pre-roll, programmatic.
- **Budget range:** $5,000-50,000+
- **Targeting:** Broad awareness audiences
- **Expected outcome:** Brand recall, category entry point, long-term growth

### Step 3: Build the Amplification Brief

For the recommended path, provide:

**Creative Decisions:**
- Use the organic post exactly as-is? Or edit for paid (add CTA, trim length, adjust hook)?
- If editing: what to change and what NOT to change (don't kill what made it work)
- Aspect ratio / format adjustments needed per platform

**Audience Decisions:**
- Core target audience
- Lookalike source (engagers on the organic post is the best seed)
- Exclusions (existing customers if running acquisition, etc.)

**Budget Decisions:**
- Starting daily budget
- Test duration (minimum 3-5 days before judging)
- Scale triggers (if CPA is under $X or ROAS is above $X, increase budget)
- Kill triggers (if no conversions after $X spent, stop)

**Platform Decisions:**
- Which platform(s) to run paid on (usually the platform where it organically overindexed, but cross-platform amplification can work)
- Meta (Facebook + Instagram) for broadest paid reach
- TikTok Ads for younger demo
- LinkedIn Ads for B2B

### Step 4: Measurement Framework

What to track after amplification starts:

| Metric | What It Tells You | Threshold |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| **CPM (cost per 1,000 impressions)** | How cheaply you're reaching people | Below $10 is good, below $5 is great |
| **CTR (click-through rate)** | Is the creative compelling enough to act on? | Above 1% is good, above 2% is great |
| **CPA (cost per acquisition)** | What are you paying per lead/sale? | Depends on your margins |
| **ROAS (return on ad spend)** | Are you making money? | Above 3x for e-commerce, above 2x for lead gen |
| **Frequency** | How many times has each person seen it? | Above 3-4 = creative fatigue, swap |

```markdown

**Overindex Ratio:** [X]x
**Organic views:** [X] vs. norm of [X]

## Recommended Path: [A: Boost / B: Performance Ad / C: Brand Campaign]
**Reasoning:** [Why this path fits]

## Creative Brief

- **Edits needed:** [specific changes, if any]
- **Do NOT change:** [elements that made it work organically]

## Audience Brief

- **Lookalike source:** [engagers on the organic post]
- **Exclusions:** [who to exclude]

## Budget Brief
- **Starting daily budget:** $[X]
- **Test duration:** [X] days
- **Scale trigger:** [condition to increase spend]
- **Kill trigger:** [condition to stop]
- **Maximum test budget:** $[X]

## Platform(s) for Paid
- [Platform 1]: [ad format]
- [Platform 2]: [ad format, if applicable]

## Measurement
Track these KPIs daily:
- CPM: target below $[X]
- CTR: target above [X]%
- CPA: target below $[X]
- ROAS: target above [X]x
- Frequency: pause at [X]

## Next Steps
1. [ ] Set up ad campaign on [platform]
2. [ ] Upload creative
3. [ ] Configure audience
4. [ ] Set budget and schedule
5. [ ] Review performance after [X] days
6. [ ] If winning: scale per triggers above
7. [ ] If losing: send to content-kill-iterate-engine
```
